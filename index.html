Peri Peri Chicken

Peri Peri Chicken is a flavorful meal of any cut of chicken that is marinated in and also served with a homemade peri peri sauce, a hot pepper sauce from south Africa. If you’ve never enjoyed south African Peri Peri Chicken, it’s definitely a treat, especially with chicken legs.
