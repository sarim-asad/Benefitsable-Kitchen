Square Shaped Samosa

By Sadia Asad on Nov 16, 2021

Ingredients:
-All Purpose Flour 2 cups
-Salt 1/2 tsp
-Baking Powder 1 tsp
-Ghee 2 tbs
-Water
-Make Dough and apply few Drops of Oil
-Cover it for 30 minutes
-Potato 4 medium
-Salt 1 tsp
-Black Pepper 1 tsp
-Roasted Cumin 1 tsp
-Sabut Dhanya 1 tbs
-Ajwain 1/4 tsp
-Chaat Masala 1 tsp
-Chilli Flakes 1 tbs
-Heat the Oil on medium Flame
-Fry it on low Flame
-Take out when it's turn to golden brown
