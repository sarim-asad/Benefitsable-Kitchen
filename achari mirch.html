achari mirch

By Sadia Asad on Oct 7, 2021

Ingredients:
-Green Chilli 15
-Nigella Seeds 1 tbs
-Fenugreek Seeds Â½ tsp
-Mustard Seeds 1 tbs
-Fennel 2 tbs
-Cumin Seeds 1 tbs
-Chaat Masala 1 tbs
-Salt 1 tsp
-Grind All Spices
-Cut Green Chillies into the middle and Remove all Seeds
-Lemon / Vinegar 2 tbs
-Mix it well
-Fill mixture into the chillies
-Water 2 tbs
-Oil 4 to 5 tbs
-Heat Oil on medium flame
-Put filled green chillies into pan
-Fry it medium to high flame Until Color Changed
-Pour liquified Masala into pan
-Cover up 5 to 10 minutes
